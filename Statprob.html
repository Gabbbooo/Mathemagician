Final Step: Variance and Standard Deviation

σ² = Σ(x - μ)² · P(x)

(1.21)(0.2) + (0.01)(0.5) + (1.21)(0.3)

Lesson Summary

Probability must always sum to 1
Mean tells the expected value
Variance measures spread
Std Dev measures typical distance from mean
